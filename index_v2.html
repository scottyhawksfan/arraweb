  • About ARRA
    ARRA is one of the best and largest drawing classic rock cover bands in the Chicagoland area, and has been for the past 2 decades! We were also voted BEST CLASSIC ROCK BAND in the Nitelife Best of the Burbs Contest for the 5th year in a row!

    By performing the biggest hits by the biggest bands of all time, with precision and excellence... it has been,and continues to be, the secret to our success. Our unparalleled vocal harmonies and incredible musicianship is also what has brought us to being one of the most sought after club and festival bands in the Chicago area markets.
